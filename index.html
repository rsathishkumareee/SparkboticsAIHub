About Us

A robotics & AI hub built for hands-on learners.

Sparkbotics AI Hub is an educational technology institute based in Vadavalli, Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, specialising in robotics, artificial intelligence and STEM skill development. We serve students and working professionals who want to move beyond theory into practical coding, automation and machine-building.

Practical, project-first teaching

For every stage of the journey

Programs are structured for school students, college students and working professionals alike, with beginner through advanced tracks in robotics, AI and IT fundamentals.
